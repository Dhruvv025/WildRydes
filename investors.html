Bharat Communications and Technology Corporation Ltd

Global Communications Provider

BCTC was formed in 2008 to hold the telecommunications services, media, and IT businesses of Bharat Communications Ltd, an Indian multinational mass media and telecommunications company. BCTC provides broadband, subscription television services, fixed telephone, and mobile services across 20 countries and 3 continents.

Mumbai Ventures

Venture Capital Firm

Backed by decades of experience in India’s startup ecosystem, Mumbai Ventures partners with visionary entrepreneurs. Founded to support early-stage founders, it focuses on scalable, market-disrupting ideas in AI, fintech, healthtech, and sustainable innovations. Mumbai Ventures values people first, not just investments.

The Incubator Hub

Accelerator

The Incubator Hub is a leading Indian accelerator nurturing innovations in agritech, renewable energy, and deep tech. With a model promoting sustainable, fairtrade, and socially responsible startups, it has helped shape the next wave of Indian unicorns. We look forward to working with you.
